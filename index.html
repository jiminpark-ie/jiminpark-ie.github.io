About

Scheduling research for intelligent, human-connected systems.

Jimin Park is a Ph.D. candidate in Industrial and Systems Engineering at KAIST, working with MSSLAB. Her research centers on technology and engineering methods that connect with people, especially in production and scheduling environments.

Her current interests include interactive scheduling, multi-objective scheduling, reinforcement learning for production systems, and LLM-assisted decision support for manufacturing and supply chain operations.
