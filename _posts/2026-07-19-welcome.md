Welcome! This is the first post on my site's blog. I'll use this space for notes
on scheduling, reinforcement learning, LLMs, and research life.

## How to add a new post

To publish a post, add a Markdown file to the `_posts/` folder with a file name
in the form `YYYY-MM-DD-a-short-title.md`, and start it with this header:

```yaml
---
layout: post
title: Your post title
date: 2026-07-19 10:00:00+0900
description: One line shown under the title
tags: scheduling llm
categories: research
---
```

Everything below the second `---` is the body, written in Markdown. Once you
commit the file, the site rebuilds and the post appears here automatically.

You can write **bold**, _italic_, [links](https://jiminpark-ie.github.io/), lists,
code blocks, images, and math ($E = mc^2$).

_Feel free to delete this post once you've published your own._
